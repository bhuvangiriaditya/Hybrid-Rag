Answer F1

Why chosen: Measures overlap between generated answers and ground truth while allowing partial credit. Useful for open-ended answers.

Calculation: precision = |overlap|/|pred|, recall = |overlap|/|gt|, F1 = 2·precision·recall/(precision+recall).

Interpretation: Higher is better; 1.0 is perfect lexical match.

Contextual Precision

Why chosen: Measures grounding by checking how much of the answer appears in retrieved context, indicating faithfulness.

Calculation: CP = |answer tokens ∩ context tokens| / |answer tokens|.

Interpretation: Higher is better; low values suggest hallucination risk.
